Cousine from Lecce, Italy


Because of the rich Baroque architectural monuments found in the city, Lecce is commonly nicknamed "The Florence of the South".
The city also has a long traditional affinity with Greek culture going back to its foundation;
Our dishes are intended to show that mixture between the Greek and Italian culture that is lived in Lecce with the most exquisite flavors of the region
